This is SaltCast.

Forecasting salt exposure to protect water resources and infrastructure.

SaltCast is a salinity management tool that helps protect drinking water safety, sustainable freshwater use, and infrastructure integrity under climate change. It integrates advanced hydrological and oceanographic models, AI-based algorithms, and ion-specific measurements to deliver actionable information.

SaltCast Delivers.

Salinity Exposure Forecasting

Forecasting salinity, specific conductance, chloride, and sodium at user-specified locations from hours to decades

Corrosion Exposure Forecasting

Forecasting chloride to sulfate mass ratios, pH, alkalinity, and hardness trends from acute to chronic conditions.

Why Now?

Tidal rivers and estuaries are becoming saltier, impacting freshwater users and infrastructure operations. Accelerating sea level rise, deepening droughts, and increased water consumption are driving saltwater intrusion from the ocean, while changes in land-use are leading to freshwater salinization.

Why SaltCast?

SaltCast uses a cutting-edge watershed-estuary model to simulate the transport and fate of major salt ions. The model output is combined with artificial intelligence algorithms to identify management strategies. Read papers from our leading scientists here.
